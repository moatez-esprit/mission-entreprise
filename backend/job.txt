Job Title: Embedded Systems Engineer
We are looking for a motivated Embedded Engineer to design, develop, and test firmware for microcontroller-based systems. 
The ideal candidate has solid experience in C/C++, real-time systems, and low-level driver development. 
You'll work closely with hardware and software teams to bring innovative embedded solutions to life.

Requirements:

Proficiency in C/C++ for embedded systems

Experience with microcontrollers (e.g., STM32, NXP)

Knowledge of communication protocols (UART, SPI, I2C, CAN)

Familiarity with RTOS or bare-metal programming

Nice to have: Python, embedded Linux, CI/CD tools, automotive or IoT experience.
